Your Security is Our Priority

Military-Grade Encryption

All your data is encrypted with AES-256, the same standard used by banks and governments. Even if someone accessed our database, they couldn't read your information.

Password Protection

We never store your actual password. Instead, we use bcrypt with a unique salt for each user to create an irreversible hash. This means not even our admins can see your password.

Secure Database

Your personal information is stored in an isolated database with strict access controls. Regular security audits ensure your data remains protected at all times.

Two-Factor Authentication

Enable 2FA for an extra layer of security. Even if someone gets your password, they won't be able to access your account without your second authentication method.
